Prepare For Your Bowenwork Session

  1. Please wear loose or stretchy clothing. You may wish to bring along a pair of lightweight or stretchy shorts to put on before your session.
  2. Plan to take it easy for the rest of the day after your appointment. A leisurely walk or swim is recommended. Avoiding overworking your body to allow it to continue repairing.
  3. While receiving Bowenwork, please do not receive other body work such as chiropractic or massage. If you must receive other forms of bodywork, please schedule your appointments no less than five days after your Bowenwork sessions or on alternate weeks.
  4. It is recommended new clients schedule two sessions, one week apart. in order to set the foundation of the work. Afterward, you are free to create your own appointment schedule based on your progress.
